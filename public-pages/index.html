Who We Are

FitnessHub is a professional fitness center committed to providing a complete and advanced training experience. Our mission is to build a strong and qualified team capable of delivering the highest level of service to our members.

What Is This Platform?

This website was developed to modernize the hiring process inside FitnessHub. Through an organized evaluation form, applicants can submit their information and receive an instant score that reflects their readiness, skills, and suitability for the job.

How It Works

  • Applicants fill out the online evaluation form.
  • The system calculates a score automatically based on the answers.
  • Admin reviews all submissions and selects the best candidates.
  • High-scoring applicants get invited to the final interview.
